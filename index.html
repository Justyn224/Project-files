Classic West African Jollof Rice

A vibriant, one pot rice dish cooked in a flavourful tomato and pepper base. Known for it's smoky aroma and bold spices, it is a staple at celebration

id=ingredients

ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain parboiled rice
  • 1 can (400g) tomato paste
  • 3 large red bell peppers (Tatashe)
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (Atarodo)
  • 2 large onions finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp thyme and 2 curry powder
id=Instuctions

Instructions

  1. Blend the red bell peppers, scotch bonnets, and one onion until smooth
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and saute the second onion until translucent
  3. Add the tomato paste and fry for 5-7 minutes to remove the sour taste
  4. Pour in the blended pepper mix and cook until the oil rises to the top
  5. Stir in the washed rice, chicken stock, and spices. Cover tightly and simmer on low heat for 30 minutes
